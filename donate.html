Your Support Matters to Us!
Powered by Eventbrite
Techstravaganza is a nonprofit event that is mainly supported by sponsorships, donations, and grants. This money goes toward lab materials and our new transportation program.

For schools with high interest, with priority given to Title I elementary schools, we are providing buses to and from their school and TJ for the day of the event. Accessibility to all is one of our main goals and we hope that this new transportation system will help get more interested kids to Techstravaganza!

If you are interested in making a donation or sponsoring, please email us directly at twistcoordinators@gmail.com or the TJ Partnership Fund STEM Outreach Coordinator, jritt@fcps.edu.

Donations of over $250 qualify as sponsorships and will qualify your logo on our promotional materials and website. STEM prize donations (drones, lab kits, etc.) are also welcome for our raffle!


Every dollar counts! Each lab varies in cost from $20 to $200 so each dollar goes a long way. For smaller donations, feel free to donate through our Eventbrite Page!

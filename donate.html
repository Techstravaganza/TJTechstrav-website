Your Support Matters to Us!
      Vigorous fundraising efforts are essential to the success of our program. Client fees cover only a fraction of the total costs of program operation, and donations are needed to cover the costs of maintaining our herd and offering innovative and effective programs to our clients. Please consider a tax-deductible donation to sponsor a horse, to offer programming to survivors of trauma and abuse, or to provide scholarship assistance to a family in need. Every contribution Bridle Paths receives goes directly to the support of our clients and our dedicated program horses.

      Bridle Paths is a certified 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Please make your tax deductible donation through our safe and secure PayPal portal to the right or e-mail us for more information on what you can to do to help our organization grow and flourish.
      Instead of (or in addition to) making a monetary donation, you can also donate your time! Volunteers are critical to our program, and can help in a variety of ways. Lesson volunteers can work in lessons as horse leaders and side walkers, and can assist with preparing horses for lessons. Barn volunteers help with horse care and facility operation. Training is available, though horse experience is a welcome asset!

      Volunteers also provide support with special projects and administrative matters, including photography, special programs and events, and marketing and publicity.
